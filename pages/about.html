Travel, Designed With Intention

Queen Afrique Safaris curates thoughtful journeys across Africa - grounded in respect for land, culture, and time.

A Slower Way to See Africa: Land & Legacy

We believe the most profound encounters are found through presence and patience—not crowded itineraries. Whether you are tracking the red elephants of Tsavo or sharing a fire with the Hadzabe, our journeys move at the rhythm of the land. We prioritize genuine connection over checklists, ensuring you aren't just a spectator, but a guest of the wilderness and its custodians.

Every safari is an intentional bridge between Africa’s iconic wildlife and its vibrant cultures. From the Great Migration in the Maasai Mara to the ancient maritime traditions of the El Molo on Lake Turkana, we create space to listen to the stories the land holds. This is travel that honors the delicate balance between preserving endangered species and empowering the indigenous communities who protect them.

By choosing this slower path, you become a catalyst for change. Every booking directly supports a dual mission: funding critical anti-poaching initiatives and providing essential resources like health and education supplies to the communities we visit. At Queen Afrique Safaris, we prove that the most unforgettable adventures are those that give back to both the earth and its people.

Our Philosophy

Our vision is to set the global standard for conservation-driven travel. We act as a catalyst for transformative tourism where every journey creates a measurable, positive impact for ecosystems across Kenya and Africa. This philosophy ensures that our travelers are not just observers, but active participants in a movement that proves tourism can be a powerful force for good.

Inclusivity is a vital pillar of our green tourism model. We believe that the wonders of Africa should be accessible to everyone, which is why we offer specially designed accommodations and adaptive equipment for travelers of all abilities. By breaking down barriers to entry, we ensure that meaningful, sustainable adventure is a universal right

Ultimately, every journey we lead tells a story of hope and connection. By choosing to travel with us, you are supporting a future where wildlife thrives, cultures are celebrated, and indigenous communities are respected. We are dedicated to proving that the most unforgettable adventures are those that give back to the world.

What Guides Us

Environmental Stewardship

We minimize our environmental footprint by partnering exclusively with eco-certified lodges, utilizing fuel-efficient vehicles, and eliminating single-use plastics across all our tours.

Community Empowerment

We ensure that the economic benefits of tourism stay local by employing native guides, sourcing supplies from community enterprises, and providing fair wages and ethical working conditions.

Conservation Action

Every booking directly funds critical wildlife protection initiatives, including anti-poaching patrols, habitat restoration, and conservation research partnerships to protect endangered species.
